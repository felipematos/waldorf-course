To add actual music to your Waldorf course:

1. Download or create Waldorf-style music files (MP3 format)
2. Name them according to the list in README.md
3. Place them in this /public/sounds folder
4. The music player will automatically use them

The music player features:
- Auto-plays when entering a page (if browser allows)
- Remembers user's on/off preference
- Smooth fade in/out transitions
- Different music for each chapter
- Visual feedback with animated music waves
- Rotating icon when music is playing

Current implementation:
- Music toggle button appears in bottom-right corner
- Click to turn music on/off
- Preference saved in localStorage
- Works across all pages
